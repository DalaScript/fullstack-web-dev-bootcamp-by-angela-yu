The Best Movies According to Dala

My top 3 movies of all-time

I Am Legend

A powerful mix of action and emotion. I love the post-apocalyptic vibe and Will Smith's performance is unforgettable.

The Maze Runner (all 3 seasons)

Fast-paced and full of mystery. I enjoyed the intense survival story and how the plot kept getting deeper.

The Prestige

Mind-blowing plot twists. I love movies that make you think, and this one had me hooked until the end.
